Cloud-9 Sports Leadership

Usama Ikram

Cheif Executive Officer

Usama Ikram is the CEO of Cloud-9 Sports! and his main role
is to serve the board of directors.

Usama's journey started when he was just 12 years old.
He was inspired from the idea from his father who was a big
sports fan. He came up with the idea to start a business where
much of the sports merchandise would come from around the world and sold domestcially.

He graduated from the University of Laurier with a major
in Finance and Business. He was able to continue his
journey after receiving his master in Business from Harvard.
By the age of 26, he started Cloud-9 Sports! after
travelling around the world to purchase top quality sporting items. Today Cloud-9 is one of the most sucessful sport stores in the world with yearly revenue of over $10 billion.
