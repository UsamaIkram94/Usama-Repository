Cloud-9 Sports Leadership

Robert Martin

Cheif Operating Officer

Robert Martin is the COO of Cloud-9 Sports! and his main role
is to serve the operations side of the Business.

Martin's journey started when he was just 12 years old.
He was inspired from the idea from his father who too was a
Operating Officer.

He graduated from the University of Laurier with a major
in Finance and Business. He was able to continue his
journey after receiving his master in Business from Harvard.
At the age of 36, he joined Cloud-9 Sports! as a COO and is
known to be one of the most sucessful people in the industry.
