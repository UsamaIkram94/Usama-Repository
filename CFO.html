Christina Kim

Cheif Financial Officer

Christina Kim is the CFO of Cloud-9 Sports! and her main role
is to serve the board of directors.

Kim's journey started when she was just 15 years old.
She was inspired from the idea from her father who was a
a finance major. Following her work exerience and knowledge she was able to join the finance team of Cloud-9 Sports!.

She graduated from the University of Laurier with a major
in Finance and Business. She was able to continue her
journey after receiving a master in Finance from MIT.
She has been part of the team for over 15 years.
