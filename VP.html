Cloud-9 Sports Leadership

Brian Hennings

Vice President

Brian Hennings is the Vice president of Cloud-9 Sports! and his main role
is to serve the board of directors.

Brian's journey started when he was just 12 years old.
He was inspired from the idea from his father who was the
president of student affairs.

He graduated from the University of Laurier with a major
in Finance and Business. He was able to continue his
journey after receiving his master in Business from Harvard.
At the age of 40, he was promoted to VP of Cloud-9 Sports!
